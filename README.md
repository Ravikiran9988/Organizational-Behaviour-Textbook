# Organizational Behaviour – Full Textbook (HTML)

## 📘 Overview

This project is a **complete Organizational Behaviour (OB) textbook** designed as a single-page HTML document.

It covers all **5 units of the syllabus** in a structured, easy-to-read format with clean UI and modern styling.

---

## 📂 File Included

* `ob-full-textbook.html` – Main HTML file containing the full content and styling

---

## 📚 Syllabus Coverage

The content is based on the standard **B.Tech Organizational Behaviour syllabus (R22)**:

### Unit I – Organizational Behaviour

* Definition, Nature and Scope
* Need and Importance
* OB Models (Autocratic, Custodial, Supportive, Collegial)
* Organizational Climate
* Organizational Structure

---

### Unit II – Individual Behaviour

* Personality (Big Five, Type A & B)
* Learning Theories (Classical, Operant, Social)
* Perception and Perceptual Errors
* Emotional Intelligence
* Attitudes and Behaviour Modification

---

### Unit III – Group Behaviour

* Types of Groups
* Stages of Group Development
* Team Building
* Communication
* Group Decision Making

---

### Unit IV – Leadership and Power

* Leadership Concepts
* Leaders vs Managers
* Leadership Styles
* Leadership Theories
* Power and Politics

---

### Unit V – Dynamics of OB

* Organizational Culture
* Job Satisfaction
* Organizational Change
* Resistance to Change
* Work Stress and Work-Life Balance

---
